My Education

Degree

The Bachelor of Islamic Banking at UiTM Kampus Kota Bharu, Kelantan, stands out as a premier program for several reasons. Firstly, it provides a comprehensive and specialized curriculum designed to equip students with a deep understanding of both conventional banking principles and Islamic finance. The program integrates core banking subjects with Shariah law, Islamic banking operations, Islamic investment, and Takaful (Islamic insurance), ensuring that graduates are well-versed in the unique aspects of Islamic finance. This dual focus not only broadens students' knowledge but also enhances their versatility and employability in various sectors of the financial industry. Furthermore, in Semester 4 I have participated in the Global Outsourcing Talent Boot Camp program organized by the UBM599 course. This course has taught me a lot in finding a job in the digital workforce.
